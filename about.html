about

femmezon is an inclusive e-commerce website that only features women-owned or non-binary-owned businesses. this name combines the french word for woman, "femme", and the name of the most well-known e-commerce conglomerate, "amazon". the business world tends to be male-dominated, so our mission is to give a platform to lesser-known, but equally important and talented business owners.

to see your business here, please send us an email at the address given under "contact"
